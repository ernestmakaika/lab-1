In the Spring Semester of the 2019-2020 Academic Year, Ernest worked on a semester-long project in which he designed
a database for We Are Family Foundation. The database was developed to keep track of the current Global Teen Teaders,
their projects and details about the social ventures that the Global Teen Leaders run. Specifically,
the database was designed to perform the following:

  • Keep track of all social entrepreneurship ventures run by the GTLs
  • Provide available funding opportunities for the GTLs
  • Track the progress made by each Global Teen Leader during the entire mentorship process.
  • Keep track of all coalition partners, mentors and other industry
    professionals who are involved in the Three Dot Dash Program.
